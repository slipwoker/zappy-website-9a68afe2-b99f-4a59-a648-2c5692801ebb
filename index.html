Commercial lighting upgrade and load balancing

Problem: A retail space experienced uneven lighting distribution and frequent circuit overloads during peak hours. The existing system was inefficient, with outdated fluorescent fixtures consuming excessive power and creating an unprofessional customer environment.

Safety Approach: Our certified electrician team performed a full electrical load audit and identified imbalanced circuits. We designed a phased upgrade plan that maintained business operations while installing LED lighting and redistributing loads across properly rated circuits with enhanced breaker protection.

Result: 40% reduction in lighting energy consumption with uniform illumination throughout the space. Load balancing eliminated circuit trips entirely, and the new LED system provides superior light quality. The upgrade qualified for energy efficiency incentives and included a 5-year warranty.

Completed over 3 weekends to minimize business disruption
